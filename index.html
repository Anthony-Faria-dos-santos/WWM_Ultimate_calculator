Core Capabilities

Forge Your Perfect Build

Every stat, every modifier, every rotation — calculated with mathematical precision rooted in the game's true combat formulas.

⚔️

DPS Simulation

Full rotation-based DPS calculation with critical hit, affinity, and multiplier stacking. Simulates real combat scenarios with frame-accurate timing.

🛡️

Gear Comparison

Side-by-side armor comparison with live stat deltas. Instantly see how swapping a single piece affects your entire build's performance.

📊

Stat Breakdown

Deep dive into every modifier: base attack, crit rate, crit damage, affinity, penetration, and elemental bonuses — all visualized.

🎯

Build Optimizer

Automatic suggestions for stat allocation based on your weapon type, inner ways, and preferred playstyle. Min-max with confidence.

🌀

Inner Ways & Skills

Full integration of Inner Ways (内功) bonuses and skill trees. See how each path affects your total output in real-time.

🌏

Multilingual Data

Complete trilingual lexicon — Chinese (CN), English (EN), French (FR) — so every term matches your game client's language.
